About Smart Sprout

Smart Sprout is made for people who love having plants in their homes but simply don't have the time or consistency to care for them.

Our smart system keeps your plants healthy, hydrated, and growing in perfect indoor conditions – all automatically. With Smart Sprout, you get the wellbeing benefits of plants like fresher air and a calmer mind, without needing patience or a green thumb.

Whether you're working full time or always on the go, Smart Sprout fits into your lifestyle and brings nature inside – effortlessly.
